“NEXT GEN”

AI Design

For the latest issue of Next Gen Magazine, I collaborated with Lessico Familiare on a series of AI-generated artworks developed from the brand’s garments. The editorial was produced with the support of tinyidols, which curated and led the project by connecting established brands with emerging designers. Together, they explored how technological innovation can reshape creative processes and open new possibilities for contemporary fashion..
